(4th year)

Major: Electrical Engineering
Goal: Hey! Being a Vietnamese-American, I acknowledge my birthright to be able to thrive off the opportunities the United States has to offer. Unlike the United States, Vietnam needs more leaders in this field. The main problem is their unsustainably designed buildings. It takes more electricity to produce the same amount of work as their neighboring countries like China which, ironically, has more infrastructure. I am pursuing this field to contribute to solving the world’s biggest challenge, starting with Vietnam.
